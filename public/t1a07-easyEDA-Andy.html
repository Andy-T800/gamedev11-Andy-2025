What is JLCPCB and what is it used for?

JLCPCB is a PCB manufacturing and assembly service that allows users to create custom circuit boards quickly and affordably.
It offers services like PCB fabrication, SMT assembly, and component sourcing, making it ideal for both prototyping and small production runs.
Their platform is known for fast turnaround, integration with design tools, and low costs, which makes it especially popular among hobbyists and engineers.

What is easyEDA and what is it used for?

EasyEDA is an online tool used to design electronic circuits and printed circuit boards (PCBs).
It allows users to draw schematics, create PCB layouts, and simulate circuits all in one place.
Since it connects directly with JLCPCB, users can go from design to manufacturing in just a few clicks.
It’s beginner-friendly and great for collaborative or cloud-based electronics projects.

What is RAKWIRELESS RAK3112 and what is it used for?

The RAK3112 is a compact IoT module that combines LoRa, WiFi, and Bluetooth connectivity with an ESP32-S3 microcontroller.
It’s designed for low-power applications like remote sensors, mesh networks, and edge AI processing.
With built-in memory and multiple wireless options, it supports versatile communication and local data processing in a small, efficient package.
